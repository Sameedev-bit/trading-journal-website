Privacy

This demo build stores everything — trades, notes, screenshots, expenses, and settings — in your own browser's local storage. No account is created, no data is transmitted to a server, and closing your browser does not share anything with anyone. Clearing your browser's site data (or using the "Reset demo data" link in the app sidebar) permanently deletes your journal. If you rely on this demo for real journaling, export or back up anything you can't afford to lose, since browser storage can be cleared by the browser at any time.
