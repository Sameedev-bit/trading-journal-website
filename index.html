Everything a prop trader's journal needs

Twelve connected workspaces — from broker sync and prop-firm rule tracking to the expense line most journals forget.

⇄

Automatic trade sync

Connect Tradovate or NinjaTrader style logins and trades flow in on their own — no spreadsheets, no copy-paste. Backfill older history one date range at a time. (Simulated in this demo build.)

▤

Multi-broker, multi-firm

Evaluations, funded accounts, sims and manual journals side by side. Filter any stat by account, watch balance and distance-to-drawdown per account.

◫

Stats that matter

Win rate, profit factor, R factor, streaks, largest days — on a monthly calendar with weekly totals, a cumulative equity curve, and per-day or per-trade views.

☀

Prep & review ritual

Pre-market prep, live checklist and day recap every weekday; weekly prep on Sunday, weekly recap on Saturday. Bias accuracy and plan adherence are scored automatically.

☑

Strategy playbooks

Define strategies with rule sections and "X of Y required" thresholds. Every trade review turns your playbook into a checklist, plus tags that feed straight into stats filters.

¤

Expense trackingNew

Data feeds, platforms, eval fees, resets — recurring subscriptions auto-log themselves each cycle, one-time costs take seconds. Net P&L after expenses shows what you actually kept.

⛨

Prop-firm rule trackerNew

Trailing drawdown floor, daily loss limit, profit target and the consistency rule — scored live per account, with red alerts the moment a rule breaks.

◔

Insights & psychologyNew

Expectancy, payoff ratio, and your edge by hour, weekday, symbol and hold time — plus what FOMO and revenge trades actually cost, and whether your confidence predicts anything.

⇪

CSV import & export

Bring real trade history in with a column-mapping importer (duplicates skipped automatically), and export trades or expenses back out any time. Your data is never locked in.

✎

Deep trade review

Notes that autosave, up to three screenshots per trade, pyramid detection, and one-click reuse of a review across same-day trades in your other accounts.

＋

Manual entry & more

Hand-log trades with a live derived P&L preview, print a clean monthly report for your mentor, and keep process goals honest with streak tracking.

⛨

Your data stays yours

Runs 100% in your browser — trades, notes and screenshots live in local storage. Light and dark themes, and a one-click reset back to the sample dataset.
